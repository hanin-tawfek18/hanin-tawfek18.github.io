* About *

A group at Helwan University's college of computer science and artificial intelligence established this website in 2024. It seeks to offer useful materials from many organizations for programming and technology courses. The website's goal is to be freely accessible and to spread its diverse information around the world.


* partnership *

We are proud to collaborate with an esteemed network of partners who share our vision for advancing programming education. Through their support and expertise, we have been able to deliver high-quality courses and resources to learners around the globe. These valued partners represent a diverse range of organizations, including educational institutions, industry leaders, and community advocates. Together, we are making a meaningful impact on the future of technology by empowering individuals with the skills they need to succeed in the digital age.
